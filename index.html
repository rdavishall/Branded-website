Who We Are

Built & Branded in the Prairies

Both of the owners of Branded Energy Solutions were raised in rural communities, located 45 minutes North of Calgary. Bryson was raised on a cattle and grain farm near Balzac. Cameron grew up around Beiseker. Together, both of the owners have built their business with the fundamental values that they each retained in their rural roots: Honesty, Hard work, Integrity. Branded Energy Solutions was incorporated in 2020. Our low overhead business model allows us to adapt to field situations efficiently and effectively. Branded Energy Solutions provides seamless in-field services, with engineering and project management support and operations. This level of service stems from the values and core principles founded and implemented in the company. Our owner grew up on one of Alberta’s cornerstone ranches. Thus Branded Energy Solutions understands how important ranchers and farmers land is to them. “Until it’s yours, you don’t really appreciate it”. At Branded Energy Solutions, our employees know the importance of working on sensitive farm and ranching operations. Our background allows for open communication with farmers, ranchers, and landowners who have abandoned wells on their property.

Mission Statement

Branded Energy Solutions’ vision is to offer end-to-end services in the asset retirement industry. Our continued reinvestment in technology and education have proven to maximize our ability to tackle dynamic projects.

Vision Statement

To continue to assist Western Canadian energy producers in reducing their liabilities, through a safe, environmentally-secure, and strategic solution.
